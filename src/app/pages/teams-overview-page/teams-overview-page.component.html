STGJ SCOUTS TEAMS

In STGJ scouts, all the members are divided into 4 main teams based on their age. In the younger ages, the members are even divided by their genders. Our teams are Louveteaux, Louvettes, Aventuriers and Pioneers.

Louveteaux

Age: Grade 4, Grade 5, Grade 6.

Gender: Boys.

Earned Skills: Knots, Gadgets, First Aid, Navigation and Survival Skills.

Louvettes

Age: Grade 4, Grade 5, Grade 6.

Gender: Girls.

Earned Skills: Knots, Gadgets, First Aid, Navigation and Survival Skills.

Aventuriers

Age: Sec 1, Sec 2, Sec 3..

Gender: Boys & Girls.

Earned Skills: Advanced Knots, Advanced Gadgets, Cooking, Tents and Survival Skills.

Pioneers

Age: Sec 4, Sec 5.

Gender: Boys & Girls.

Earned Skills: Advanced Knots, Advanced Gadgets, Cooking, Tents, Cutting Woods and Survival Skills.
