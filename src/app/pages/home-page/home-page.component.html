FAQ's

Q: How can my kid join St. George and St. Joseph Scouts ?

A: St. George and St. Joseph Scouts will open the registration for the new members in August 2024 for the next year 2022-2025. The registration is only for Grade 4 kids. For other ages, all the applications will be in a waiting list. The decision depends on the availability in the scouts team in the current year.
